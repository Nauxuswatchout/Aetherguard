Be Kind Online – Stop Cyberbullying!

1. Know What Cyberbullying Looks Like

  • 📱 Sending mean or scary messages
  • 📸 Posting embarrassing photos without permission
  • 💬 Leaving hurtful comments on posts or games
  • 🎭 Pretending to be someone else to trick or hurt you

2. Always Be Kind

  • 😊 Say nice things
  • 🎉 Encourage your friends
  • 🤝 Help others feel welcome

3. Don't Fight Back

  • 🙅‍♂️ Don't argue
  • 🛑 Don't say mean things back
  • 🚫 Don't join in

4. Tell a Grown-up You Trust

🗣️ Show the messages to a parent, teacher, or trusted adult. They can help block the bully and report it.

5. Protect Your Feeling

  • 🔒 Log out
  • 🧘 Take a break
  • 🎨 Do something you love!

You Are a Cyber Safety Hero!

🌍 Every kind word and action makes the internet safer for everyone.
