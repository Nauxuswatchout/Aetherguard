Bella the Bunny and the Mystery Photo

Bella the Bunny lived in a grassy meadow with her brother.

1

One afternoon, Bella chatted with new friends online.

2

Someone named “CoolCarrot123” sent a photo.

3

“Click to see a funny bunny dance!” the message said.

4

Bella giggled. “That sounds fun!” She reached to click.

5

But her brother shouted, “Wait! We don’t know them!”

6

Bella paused. “But it’s just a funny video… right?”

7

They asked Dad Bunny. He checked the message.

8

“This file could hide a virus,” he warned. “Don’t open it.”

9

Bella gasped. “A virus? Like... the sneezy kind?”

10

Dad smiled, “It makes your tablet sick, not you!”

11

They blocked CoolCarrot123 and reported the message.

12

Bella felt proud. “We stopped the trick in time!”

13

Now Bella says, “Don’t open files from strangers — ever!”
