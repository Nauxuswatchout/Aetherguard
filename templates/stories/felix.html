Felix the Fox and the Fake Contest

Felix the Fox lived in a treehouse near Berry Hill.

1

One day, he checked his email on his shiny laptop.

2

“Congratulations!” the message said. “You won a scooter!”

3

Felix gasped, “Wow! I never even joined a contest!”

4

The message said, “Click here and fill your details.”

5

Felix clicked. The page asked for name and password.

6

He typed fast, dreaming about riding the scooter.

7

Later, he tried to log in — but his account was gone!

8

“Oh no!” he cried. “They tricked me and stole everything!”

9

He told Owl, the wise forest teacher, right away.

10

Owl said, “Real contests don’t ask for passwords.”

11

They reported the email and changed Felix’s passwords.

12

Felix felt safe again, but he stayed more careful now.

13

Now he teaches friends: “If it’s too good — it’s fake!”
